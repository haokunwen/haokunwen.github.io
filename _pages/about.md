Short Bio
---   
I'm currently a Ph.D. student in the [School of Computer Science and Technology](http://cs.hitsz.edu.cn/), [Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen)](https://www.hitsz.edu.cn/index.html), under the supervision of Prof. [Liqiang Nie](https://liqiangnie.github.io/index.html) and Prof. [Xuemeng Song](https://xuemengsong.github.io/). I am also a joint Ph.D. student in the [School of Data Science](https://www.sdsc.cityu.edu.hk/), [City University of Hong Kong](https://www.cityu.edu.hk/), under the supervision of Prof. [Xiangyu Zhao](https://www.cityu.edu.hk/).  My research interests include multimedia computing and information retrieval.
